Recovery

Concussion Management Tips

  • Get plenty of sleep at night, and rest during the day.
  • Avoid activities that are physically demanding or require a lot of concentration.
  • Gradually return to your normal activities, but don't push yourself if symptoms return.
  • Avoid alcohol consumption.
  • Eat well-balanced meals and stay hydrated.
  • Do not drive until you have been cleared by a healthcare professional.
  • Avoid screen time (TV, computer, smartphone) if it worsens your symptoms.
  • Follow your healthcare provider's instructions carefully.
  • Be patient - recovery times can vary from person to person.

Remember, these are general tips. Always follow the specific advice given by your healthcare provider.
